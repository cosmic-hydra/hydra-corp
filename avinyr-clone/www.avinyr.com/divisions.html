📈 Division 01

Hedge Fund

Our AI-native hedge fund operates market-neutral strategies across equities, derivatives, and commodities. Every signal is generated, validated, and executed by AI — eliminating human latency and cognitive bias from the alpha loop.

  • Statistical Arbitrage — Cross-asset cointegration models identifying pricing inefficiencies at microsecond resolution
  • Momentum + Reversal — Dual-regime trend-following with adaptive lookback windows tuned per asset class
  • Options Volatility — AI-priced vol surface arbitrage capturing dispersion and skew opportunities
  • Risk Engine — Real-time drawdown control, correlation monitoring, and automated position unwinding
  • Market Microstructure — Order flow toxicity detection, VWAP/TWAP execution optimisation

📊 Division 02

Beta Fund

The Beta Fund delivers systematic, AI-optimised market exposure at scale. Rather than passive index replication, our AI continuously tilts factor weights based on regime detection, rebalancing in real-time to capture smart beta premium while minimising costs.

  • Smart Beta Engine — AI-driven factor rotation across value, momentum, quality, low-vol, and size premia
  • Index Replication — Sub-basis-point tracking error through ML-optimised sampling and hedging
  • Regime Detection — Macro regime classification informing dynamic factor tilts
  • Cost Optimisation — Transaction cost models minimise slippage and rebalancing drag
  • ESG Overlay — AI-screened sustainable factor tilts without sacrificing return profile
🏦 Division 03

Investment Bank

A fully AI-native investment bank that compresses the traditional deal lifecycle from months to days. Our systems autonomously source deals, build financial models, conduct due diligence, and generate term sheets — with human experts available for relationship closure.

  • M&A Advisory — AI target screening, synergy modelling, valuation, and integration playbooks
  • Equity Capital Markets — IPO readiness assessment, bookrunner analytics, pricing AI
  • Debt Capital Markets — Credit scoring, covenant structuring, and investor matching
  • Due Diligence AI — Document ingestion, red-flag detection, and risk scoring at scale
  • Deal Origination — Autonomous prospecting across public filings, news, and alternative data
