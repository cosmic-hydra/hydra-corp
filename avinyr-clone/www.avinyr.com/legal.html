hydra fund does not hold any registration, licence, or authorisation from the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI). In particular, it is not registered under the SEBI (Alternative Investment Funds) Regulations, 2012 as a Category I, II, or III AIF; it is not registered under the SEBI (Portfolio Managers) Regulations, 2020; and it has no authorisation to pool investor capital, provide investment advisory services, or manage discretionary or non-discretionary portfolios on behalf of any client in India.

Any references on this website to "hedge fund operations", "venture capital", "high-frequency trading", or "investment banking" describe the intended future activities of the fund once it is duly incorporated and holds all requisite regulatory approvals. These activities are not currently being conducted.

Under Indian law, soliciting investments from the public without SEBI registration is a criminal offence. hydra fund is not soliciting investments from any person through this website. The investment tiers and capital structures described on the Invest page are forward-looking illustrations of the intended fund architecture, not live or active offerings.

When pre-seed funding is secured and incorporation is completed, the company will pursue the appropriate SEBI registrations (likely Category III AIF for hedge fund strategies and Category I or II AIF for venture capital activities) and, where required, RBI approvals for cross-border capital flows before accepting any external capital.

Operational Readiness

Full Operations Are Contingent on Pre-Seed Investment

hydra fund can only become fully operational after securing pre-seed investment. This is not a stylistic caveat — it is a structural dependency. The pre-seed capital is required to fund:

  • → Legal incorporation — company formation, constitutional documents, registered office, and initial compliance costs.
  • → Regulatory filings — SEBI AIF registration fees, legal counsel for fund documents, compliance officer appointment, and KYC/AML infrastructure.
  • → Technology infrastructure — algorithmic trading platforms, data feeds, execution systems, risk engines, and cloud infrastructure for AI-driven operations.
  • → Core team — hiring of quantitative researchers, risk managers, compliance professionals, and operational staff.
  • → Initial seed capital — minimum regulatory capital requirements for AIF registration and initial strategy deployment.

If you are a potential pre-seed investor interested in supporting the formation of hydra fund, please reach out to advaith@hydracorp.dev directly. Any such conversation will be exploratory and non-binding unless and until formal documentation is in place.
